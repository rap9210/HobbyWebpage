FANTASY FOOTBALL

How does it work?

  • Before each new NFL season, each person takes their turn drafting players to their team.
  • Every week (NFL teams only play 1 game a week) you face off against a different person's team.
  • Your team's performance is quantified and adds to your week's total points.
  • If you have more points than your opponent that week you win that week.
  • The top x amount of teams go to playoffs.
  • The team that wins in playoffs wins the league.
