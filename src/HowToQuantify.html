FANTASY FOOTBALL

How to quantify performance?

Each player gets points for doing position specific actions. For example:

The Quarterback

  • Every touchdown pass is 4pts
  • Every 25 passing yards is 1pt
  • Every 10 rushing yards is 1pt
  • A fumble is -2pts

The Wide Receiver

  • Every catch is 1pt
  • Every 10 receiving yards is 1pt
  • Every touchdown is 6pt
  • A fumble is -2pts

And so on for every other position.
