FANTASY FOOTBALL

Team Management

Team management is the essence of the game.
Each team has a limited number of players they can play each week in any given position.
The goal is to analyze each player's weekly match up and determine which players will likely
produce more points than the others. Nothing is for certain. Player injuries play a big role each season.

Waivers

The waivers are the players that are not on anyone's team and can be picked up at any time before the
start of their game that week. Players that are not superstars are very unpredictable and often go un-drafted.
Keep in eye out for these prospects. They can make or break a season.

Trading

Another way to look to improve your team is to try to trade players with other teams.
Trading is a good way to improve weaknesses at certain positions. For example:

You may have too many quarterbacks and in dire need for a running back. Look for another team
that could use a quarterback and can afford to let go of a running back.

Trades should never been one sided. Any strange looking trades can be vetoed by the league to avoid
cheating/colluding.
