FANTASY FOOTBALL

Winners and Losers

Winning and losing is the biggest part of fantasy. Many leagues play for money, others play for fun, but most of them play for bragging rights. It's about laying claim to the throne, proclaiming your greatness and be as dramatic and overbearing as possible to the rest of the league. Some leagues even have punishments for the team that ended up in last place. The punishments usually involve some light public humiliation, all in good fun.
